Based on each deal, Brudge will give you a 5-minute lesson on how to get started playing the hand.

I've taught hundreds of people how to play bridge. The key is to play with fun people, and start playing immediately. Bridge is a social game, and playing with people that you like and who are all at the same level is a great way to get started.

Bridge Features

  • Four players
  • Trick-taking game (like hearts or spades)
  • Invented by Harold Vanderbilt in 1925 on a transatlantic cruise.
  • Total play time: around an hour.
  • 2-vs-2: you and your partner need to cooperate to win.
  • You need to know when to push your luck, and figure out what your opponents are holding.
  • Some people play for money to make things interesting.
